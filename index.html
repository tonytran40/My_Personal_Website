About this project

This coursework reminded me back in college when I use pomodoro technique to help me stay focused and productive. I decided to build a simple pomodoro timer as a fun project to practice my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills. The timer allows users to switch between work and break modes, start/pause/reset the timer, and visually track their progress with a progress bar. It's a great way to stay on track and manage your time effectively!
